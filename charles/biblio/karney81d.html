Currents Driven by Electron Cyclotron Waves


Bibliography entry
C. F. F. Karney and N. J. Fisch.
Currents driven by electron cyclotron waves.
Nucl. Fusion, 21(12):1549–1557, December 1981.
Princeton Univ. Rept. PPPL-1808 (July 1981) 28 pp.
Reprint: nf81
DOI: 10.1088/0029-5515/21/12/004
BibTeX Entry
Abstract
Certain aspects of the generation of steady-state currents by electron cyclotron waves are explored. A numerical solution of the Fokker-Planck equation is used to verify the theory of Fisch and Boozer and to extend their results into the non-linear regime. Relativistic effects on the current generated are discussed. Applications to steady-state tokamak reactors are considered.
