Abstract
The motion of ions in the fields B = B0z and E = E0y cos(k⊥y − ωt ) is considered. When ω >> Ωi and v⊥ > ω/k⊥, the equations of motion may be reduced to a set of difference equations. These equations exhibit stochastic behavior when E0 exceeds a threshold. The diffusion coefficient above the threshold is determined. Far above the threshold, ion Landau damping is recovered. Extension of the method to include parallel propagation is outlined.
