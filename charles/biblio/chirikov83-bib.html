  abstract =     {Motion in the stochastic layer around the separatrix
                  of a nonlinear resonance was investigated.  The
                  integral distribution function <i>F</i>(&tau;) of
                  trajectory recurrence times &tau; to the center of the
                  layer was numerically determined.  It was found that
                  the distribution <i>F</i>(&tau;) =
                  <i>A</i>&tau;<sup>&minus;<i>p</i></sup> is a power
                  function, the exponent assuming two different values:
                  for &tau; &le; &tau;<sub>0</sub>, <i>p</i> = 1/2 and
                  for &tau; &gt;&gt; &tau;<sub>0</sub>, <i>p</i> = 3/2
                  (time &tau;<sub>0</sub> is determined by the
                  characteristics for the layer).}
