  abstract =     {We consider the equation governing the mode structure
                  of Alfv&eacute;n waves.  Since the Alfv&eacute;n speed
                  is a function of density, the compressional
                  Alfv&eacute;n wave changes from evanescent to
                  propagating at some point in the plasma.  However the
                  presence of the shear Alfv&eacute;n resonance changes
                  this cutoff into a cutoff-resonance-cutoff triplet.
                  By analytically and numerically studying the
                  properties of the governing equation, we determine the
                  absorption due to the resonance and <i>Q</i> for the
                  eigenmode.  We show that heating is possible using
                  relatively high-<i>Q</i> eigenmodes.}
