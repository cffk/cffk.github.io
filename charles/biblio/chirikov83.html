Abstract
Motion in the stochastic layer around the separatrix of a nonlinear resonance was investigated. The integral distribution function F(τ) of trajectory recurrence times τ to the center of the layer was numerically determined. It was found that the distribution F(τ) = Aτ−p is a power function, the exponent assuming two different values: for τ ≤ τ0, p = 1/2 and for τ >> τ0, p = 3/2 (time τ0 is determined by the characteristics for the layer).
